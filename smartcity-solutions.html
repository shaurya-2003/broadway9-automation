Smart City Solutions

Transforming Urban Living with Smart City
Innovations

Welcome to our Smart City Solutions page. At Broadway9-Automation, we are committed to revolutionizing urban living through innovative technologies and solutions. From electric vehicle charging stations to smart garbage collection systems, solar grids, and industrial IoT solutions, we offer comprehensive smart city solutions designed to enhance efficiency, sustainability, and quality of life.

Our Solutions

Smart Garbage Collection Systems

We design advanced System on Chip (SoC) solutions, integrating multiple functions into a single chip. Our SoCs power a range of devices, optimizing performance, energy efficiency, and connectivity for diverse applications.

Electric Vehicle charging Solution

Our EV charging stations are equipped with hardware and smart software integration, enabling efficient charging management, payment processing, and remote monitoring.

Solar Grids

We design and implement solar energy systems to power public infrastructure, streetlights, and buildings, reducing reliance on traditional energy sources and lowering carbon footprint.

Industrial IoT Solutions

Our IoT solutions for industrial purposes include remote monitoring, predictive maintenance, and asset tracking, enhancing operational efficiency and productivity in manufacturing and logistics.

Process Flow

Assesment

We conduct a thorough assessment of the city's infrastructure, challenges, and goals to identify opportunities for smart city solutions.

Design & Development

Our team of engineers and developers design and develop customized solutions tailored to the city's specific needs, ensuring seamless integration and functionality.

Deployment

We deploy the solutions in collaboration with local authorities and stakeholders, providing training and support to ensure successful implementation and adoption.

Monitoring & Optimization

We continuously monitor and optimize the performance of the deployed solutions, leveraging data analytics and feedback to drive continuous improvement and maximize impact.
